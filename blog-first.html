Have Dinner with Jancis Robinson

She’s been called the world’s most respected wine critic. She’s the author of not one, but three of the wine world’s most important reference books. She’s an Officer of the British Empire and advises the British Royal Family on their wine cellar. And she’s one of the few mononymous people in wine. To most, she’s just Jancis: a towering figure in the world of wine that needs little introduction. To me, she’s a boss, collaborator, and friend.

And on October 30th, she’s inviting you to have dinner with her in San Francisco.

To celebrate the 25th Anniversary of her website, JancisRobinson.Com, she’s buying out in San Francisco, and hosting a dinner for 40 in conjunction with sommelier and restaurateur Paul Einbund and his chef Gavin Schmidt.
